Rocketry

IGNITORS

Interdisciplinary Group for Navigation, Innovation, and Thrust Optimization of Rockets – Texas A&M University


As the Structures Lead, I own the end‑to‑end structural development of a 10k ft Commercial‑Off‑The‑Shelf hybrid rocket headed to the International Rocket Engineering Competition (IREC) in June 2025. Since team formation in November 2024 we have delivered a flight‑ready prototype, secured sponsorships, and sourced manufacturing partners  — all on track for our first launch campaign.


| My Role & Scope

  • Led a 12‑person sub‑team through requirements, CAD, FEA, and manufacturing.
  • Developed an internal composite design toolbox (Python) that cut laminate sizing time by 60 %.
  • Mentored team in SolidWorks, ANSYS Mechanical, Classical Lamination Theory, and Tsai‑Wu failure envelopes.

| Analysis & Verification

  • Flight modelling – OpenRocket & RASAero II predict apogee 10,120 ft AGL @ Mach 0.98 with 1.5 cal stability margin.
  • FEA – ANSYS Static Structural on orthotropic lay‑ups shows ≥ 2.5 × limit‑load safety factor.
  • Coupon tests – 8‑ply G12/Epoxy tensile & shear coupons validate lamina inputs (±8 %).
  • Fin‑can rig – Custom screw‑jack fixture: 200 lbf compression / 150 lbf bending, 0.8 mm tip deflection.
